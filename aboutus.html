UNIAPOLis school of business

We believe in small is beautiful. Because of our size we are able to offer flexibility & personalized educational services to our students. Currently we have 15 full time and part time teachers. We maintained a student to teacher ratio of maximum 8:1.
At Uniapolis School of Business, there are no barriers between discovery, transfer and application of knowledge. We teach with our ‘heart and soul’ and service our students with our ‘blood and sweet’. We believe in full commitment & involvement when servicing our students from all walks of life and nationalities.

About us

Uniapolis School of Business was established and registered with Ministry of Education, Singapore in 1999. Currently it is registered with SkillsFuture Singapore Agencies (SSG), Singapore, (UEN:197901906W, period of registration 26 April 2021 to 25 April 2025).
Our main purpose is to offer certificate as well as diploma programs for working adults and school leavers to prepare them for a globalised world.
In line with our niche market concept of staying focus & relevant, most of our courses, if possible will be provided with language assistance for those who are weak in English language.
