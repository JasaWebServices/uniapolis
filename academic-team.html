Academic Team

As a dynamic professional institute, the responsibility for maintaining standards is shared by all members of the academic team. As instructors, faculty members transmit knowledge and the methods by which it is acquired.Members of academic team have the responsibility to preserve and transmit academic values in the learning environment and in the example they provide to students. To this end, they are expected to instill in their students a respect for integrity and a desire to behave honestly; they must also take measures to discourage student academic dishonesty.Uniapolis School of business has an ongoing commitment to maintain and encourage academic integrity
