ACCA Courses

中英文讲解

ACCA (the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants) is the global body for professional Accountants. ACCA aims to offer business-relevant, targeted combination of study and practical experience to people of application, ability, and ambition around the world who seek a rewarding career in Accounting, finance, and management. ACCA graduates will be eligible to apply for ISCA membership and ACCA membership with ACCA (UK) upon completion of the requisite work experience.

IAB LCCI L3 Group Diploma In Accounting

中英文讲解

The LCCI Diploma in Accounting and Finance course is a diploma program from the London Chamber of Commerce and Industry (LCCI), awarded by the Institute of Accountants and Bookkeepers, with online assessment. The accounting skills imparted by this diploma course are useful in the business sector.

Certified Accounting Technician

中英文讲解

The CAT Qualification is a diploma-level qualification awarded by the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA). It is a qualification designed for those who are looking to start a career in finance, certified for the skills and experience already gained in employment, or gain accelerated progression to the ACCA qualification.
