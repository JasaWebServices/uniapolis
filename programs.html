ACCA Courses

中英文讲解

ACCA (the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants) is the global body for professional Accountants. ACCA aims to offer business relevant, targeted combination of study and practical experience, to people of application, ability and ambition around the world who seeks a rewarding career in Accounting, finance and management. ACCA graduate will be eligible to apply for ICPAS membership and ACCA membership with ACCA (UK) upon completion of the requisite work experience.

The LCCI Diploma in Accounting and Finance course is a diploma program from the London Chamber of Commerce and Industry (LCCI), awarded by Pearson Education Ltd, with online assessment. The accounting skills imparted by this diploma course are useful in the business sector.

Certified Accounting Technician

中英文讲解

The CAT Qualification is a diploma level qualification awarded by the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA). It is a qualification designed for those who are looking to start career in finance, certify for the skills and experience already gained in employment or gain accelerated progression to the ACCA qualification.
