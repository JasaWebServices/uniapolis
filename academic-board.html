Academic Board

The Academic Board is to look into the academic quality and rigour of the private education institution. Its responsibilities include:Developing the policies and procedures to ensure academic quality and rigour such as:

  • Ensuring that the content and duration of the modules or subjects, as well as the entry and graduation requirements of the course are appropriate; and
  • Approving the deployment of teachers based on the requirements stipulated by the Council for Private Education
  • Facilitating the private education institution to implement and comply with the polices and procedures developed; and
  • Reviewing, at least once a year, the academic polices and procedures.
