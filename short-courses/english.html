English for Business (EFB)

中英文讲解

English for Business (EFB), Preliminary - Level 4, these qualifications are appropriate for those who wish to gain an understanding of English within a business context. The core examination is a writing and reading test in which candidates are required to complete business related tasks. These include comprehension exercises, writing letters, memos or reports and using data to complete forms or diagrams.

Optional EFB Speaking and Listening tests are available at all five levels. Candidates are able to progress from a basic understanding of English in a business contexts to a high level of proficiency.
